📅 November Events in the Philippines

Celebrations, Festivals & Public Holidays

Public Holidays

November 1

Undas – All Saints’ Day

Special Non-Working Holiday nationwide. Families visit cemeteries to honor deceased loved ones. (November 2 is traditionally observed but not always a non-working day.)

November 30

Bonifacio Day

Nationwide regular holiday honoring Andres Bonifacio.

Major Festivals & Events

November 3–5

Pintaflores Festival

Cultural

San Carlos City, Negros Occidental

Vibrant street dancing with flower-painted costumes.

November 5

El Cinco de Noviembre

Historical

Negros Occidental

Commemoration of the bloodless Negrense uprising.

November 9–11

Helobong Festival

Indigenous

Lake Sebu, South Cotabato

T’boli tribe cultural showcase.

November 19

National Fraternity Day

Civic

Nationwide

Community service, bloodletting, tree planting. Not a public holiday.

November 23

Higantes Festival

Parade

Angono, Rizal

Famous parade of giant paper-mâché figures.

Late November

La Union Surfing Kickoff

Sports

San Juan, La Union

Official opening of the peak surfing season.
