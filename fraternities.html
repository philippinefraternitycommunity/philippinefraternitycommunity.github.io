Philippine Fraternity Community

281 Fraternities Stand United
for National Fraternity Day PH

A historic show of solidarity, peace, and nation-building

In a historic display of inter-fraternal unity, 281 fraternities, sororities, and brotherhoods across the Philippines have thrown their full support behind the National Fraternity Day PH.

The Visionary Behind National Fraternity Day

Established and driven by the Philippine Fraternity Community — a decentralized grassroots alliance — National Fraternity Day continues to promote long-term inter-fraternal peace and solidarity every November 19.
