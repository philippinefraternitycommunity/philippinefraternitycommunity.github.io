About Philippine Fraternity Community

The Philippine Fraternity Community is the founder of National Fraternity Day PH. It is a decentralized, collective grassroots alliance of diverse Greek-letter organizations, fraternities, and sororities in the Philippines.

This alliance coordinates the annual nationwide celebration every November 19 and promotes inter-fraternal peace, unity, and collaborative service to the Filipino people.
