Philippine Fraternity Community

The Philippine Fraternity Community is the national grassroots alliance of Greek-letter organizations, fraternities, and sororities in the Philippines. It serves as the unifying body promoting inter-fraternal peace, brotherhood, and service to the nation.

National Fraternity Day PH

Wikidata: Q139882769

National Fraternity Day PH is the official annual observance dedicated to honoring the history, contributions, and continuing role of fraternities and sororities in the Philippines.

Instance of: Commemorative day
Observed annually: November 19
Theme: Fraternity in Peace, Unity in Purpose
Founded by: Philippine Fraternity Community

Historical Significance

November 19 marks the founding of Upsilon Sigma Phi in 1918 — the first Greek-letter fraternity in the Philippines. This date now serves as a unifying symbol for all fraternities and sororities across the country through the Philippine Fraternity Community.
