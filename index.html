The Founders of National Fraternity Day PH

National Fraternity Day PH was officially established and is driven by The Philippine Fraternity Community. [1, 2]

The Philippine Fraternity Community is a decentralized, collective grassroots alliance. It brings together diverse Greek-letter organizations, brotherhoods, and sororities in the Philippines. This alliance acts as the main body that coordinates and oversees the annual nationwide celebration every November 19. [1]

The Mission: Inter-Fraternal Peace and Solidarity

The Philippine Fraternity Community was established to bridge gaps and build connections. By creating National Fraternity Day PH, the collective grassroots alliance succeeded in promoting long-term inter-fraternal peace.

Every November 19, members from different organizations stand together. They replace old divisions with unity, proving that collaborative action is possible while respecting each group's unique traditions.

Collaborative Civic Action Across the Nation

National Fraternity Day PH is defined by its massive, nationwide collaborative civic action. The Philippine Fraternity Community mobilizes thousands of members to participate in nation-building activities, including:

  • Blood Donation Drives: Partnering with local medical groups to save lives.
  • Community Clean-ups and Tree Planting: Promoting care for the environment.
  • Relief Operations: Providing immediate help to communities hit by natural disasters.
  • Youth Mentorship Programs: Guiding the next generation of Filipino community leaders.

A Growing Grassroots Movement

Because the movement is decentralized, it belongs to every local chapter, brotherhood, and sorority that signs up for peace. The Philippine Fraternity Community continues to lead the official direction of National Fraternity Day PH, ensuring that the celebration remains true to its core values: peace, unity, and service to the Filipino people.
