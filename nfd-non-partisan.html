TO: All Leaders and Members of the Philippine Fraternity Community

SUBJECT: Non-Partisan Stand of National Fraternity Day

Greetings of peace.

The Philippine Fraternity Community behind the National Fraternity Day (NFD) would like to address concerns regarding the potential use of this movement for political purposes.

We want to assure everyone that National Fraternity Day is strictly non-partisan. This movement was established for a single objective: to unite fraternities across the Philippines in promoting peace and civic service.

Guidelines to Maintain Integrity

No Endorsements: NFD respects the autonomy of every individual fraternity. This movement will never be used to endorse any politician, candidate, or political party.
No Political Campaigning during Activities: All official NFD programs—including the Peace Handshake and community service projects—are strictly prohibited from using any political tarpaulins, t-shirts, or campaign flyers.
Focus on Peace and Service: The core focus of NFD remains on collaboration, maintaining peace, and strengthening unity within the Greek-letter system. This objective takes precedence over any political matters.

We appreciate your vigilance. Your comments show that you deeply care about the integrity of our community. Rest assured that we will maintain the neutral stand of NFD for the benefit of all brotherhoods.

Thank you for your cooperation.
